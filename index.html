Data Visualization

A visualization of ufo sightings in the U.S. with durations between 5 seconds and 5 hours, plotted by latitude and longitude and colored/sized by the log of the duration in seconds. A lighter color corresponds to a longer duration, and a darker color corresponds to a shorter one.

The dataset was the Kaggle UFO Signtings dataset. I removed the datapoints corresponding to a duration of less than 5 seconds as well as those corresponding to a duration of more than 5 hours, choosing to focus on UFO sightings between those time frames.
